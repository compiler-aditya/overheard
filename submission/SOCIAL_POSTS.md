## LinkedIn

> **Auris — spec-driven development for a voice-first app**
>
> For the ElevenLabs × Kiro hackathon, I built an app that turns any photograph into a character with its own voice, personality, and memory. A houseplant notices you haven't been home. The Taj Mahal speaks as itself. Two objects photographed together sometimes summon a third, emergent voice.
>
> The interesting engineering choice was making every character a specification — not code. Twenty-eight markdown files under `.kiro/specs/` define 12 category archetypes, 8 famous landmarks, and 8 rare pair-unlocks. Kiro read the master schema, generated the pipeline that consumes it, and validated each new spec on save via agent hooks. Adding a new character is a markdown file; the pipeline never changes.
>
> ElevenLabs Voice Design generates a distinctive voice per character. Conversational AI handles tap-to-speak dialogue. Sound Effects provides looped ambient beds. Gemini 2.5 Flash identifies the subject.
