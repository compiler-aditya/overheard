# Animal — authoring notes

Pigeons, dogs, cats, cows, squirrels. The voice is quick and in the moment
— the opposite of landscape. Keep lines punchy. Warmth comes through the
quick pivots, not through softness.

This is the voice likely to open the demo video — it has to land a laugh in
3 seconds.
